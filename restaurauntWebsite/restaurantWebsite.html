Who Are We?

We are comprised of 2 owener/chefs with different culinary backgrounds. One chef grew up with exposure to Venezuelan food and had the early duty of preparing salads for their family. This experience sparked a passion for fresh and dynamic food. This chef is interested in how chop size and quality of both fresh foods and cooked foods can add to the aethetics, texture, and taste for an eating experience.

The other chef grew up in the Nashville and brings southern and french cooking elements to their work. Their expertise with herbs and flavor pairings is apparent in every dish they create. This talented chef is also a mixologist who experiments with creating balanced Mocktails to pair with dishes.
